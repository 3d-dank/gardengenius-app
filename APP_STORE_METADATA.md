## Description

Take the guesswork out of lawn care with LawnGenius — the AI-powered lawn diagnosis app that identifies grass diseases, nutrient deficiencies, and weed infestations in seconds. Simply snap a photo of your lawn and our advanced AI agronomist analyzes it instantly, delivering a precise diagnosis with confidence scores and step-by-step treatment plans. Whether it's brown patch fungus, iron chlorosis, or crabgrass invasion, LawnGenius tells you exactly what's wrong and how to fix it.

LawnGenius uses your GPS location to provide hyper-local soil intelligence, including your USDA hardiness zone, soil temperature data, and current UV index — so your treatment recommendations are always seasonally appropriate. Get exact product names, application rates per 1,000 sq ft, and optimal timing windows so you never waste money on the wrong product or apply at the wrong time.

Track your lawn's health over time with our intelligent Lawn Health Score, calculated from your scan history. The score dynamically adjusts based on detected severity — so you can see real improvement as your treatments take effect. Every diagnosis is saved to your personal history log so you can monitor progress, compare treatments, and build a complete picture of your yard's needs across the seasons.

LawnGenius is free to start — get 3 full AI diagnoses at no cost. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited scans, priority AI processing, complete treatment history, and advanced yard mapping features. Whether you're a first-time homeowner or a lawn care enthusiast, LawnGenius gives you professional-grade agronomist insight right in your pocket.
